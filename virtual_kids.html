Digital Literacy Skills for your Kids

Students will gain essential digital literacy skills, including how to use computers, navigate the internet safely, and work with various software.

Digital Literacy Skills for your Kids

Kids will learn important skills for navigating the internet safely, protecting their personal information, and avoiding online threats.

This program covers topics such as privacy settings, recognizing cyberbullying, understanding online scams, and the importance of using strong passwords. Children will also learn how to be responsible digital citizens.

Creative Design for Kids

In this program, kids will develop creative skills by learning graphic design, video editing, and other digital arts.

Children will be introduced to design tools like Canva, Photoshop, and basic video editing software. They will work on projects like creating digital artwork, editing short videos, and designing their own posters.

Coding for Kids

Our program teaches kids how to code, empowering them to create their own digital projects and learn fundamental programming concepts.

Kids will start with the basics of programming using user-friendly languages like Scratch and Python. They will develop problem-solving skills and creativity as they build games, animations, and simple applications.
